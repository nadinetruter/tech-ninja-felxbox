Our History

Mzamomtsha Primary School started in April 2004. Headmaster,
Mr Swimphiwe Khuze joined in 2007. The school was built
in Drift Sands to help support the localcommunity by providing
an educational system for the informal settlement. The school
initially started for primary school students but now accomadates
learners up until their matric year. Our school is still developing,
as the number of students increase each year.




Our Vision

Mzamomtsha Primary School vision is to give authority
to the students to communicate fluently and coherently
and skills that will support them. The most important
values of the school:Respect, tolerance, inclusion,
excellence,integrity and diligence.

Mzamomtsha Primary School mission is to help students
